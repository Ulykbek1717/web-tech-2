Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the online store ShopLite collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit the site and place orders.

1. What data we collect
  • Contact details: name, email, phone, shipping address.
  • Order details: items, price, payment and delivery methods.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser type, cookies.
2. How we use data
  • Processing and delivering orders, order-related communications.
  • Customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Improving service quality and personalizing content.
  • Complying with legal requirements and preventing fraud.
3. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies for site functionality and analytics. You can limit cookies in your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect the operation of certain features.

4. Your rights
  • Right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • Right to correct and delete your data.
  • Right to object to processing and to restrict it.
5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries contact us: support@shoplite.com, +7 777 777 77 77 (9:00–21:00).
